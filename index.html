Who am I?

Hi, my name is Tony. I am a teacher on YouTube, teaching programming, electronics, and more. I also embark on various personal programming projects, with a small number of them available on this site. I occasionally compete in game making competitions (such as LudumDare or TriJam), and sometimes livestream programming.
