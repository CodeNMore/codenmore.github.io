How to Play:

Use WASD or the Arrow Keys to move!
Aim with your mouse!
Shoot by clicking (or holding the mouse button for continuous shooting!)
Press [SPACE] to place a bomb!
Avoid all blocks that are colors other than yours!
Touch a purple block for it to become your buddy and shoot with you!
